Help Page

Getting Started

Welcome to our image selling website! To get started, follow these steps:

  • Browse our collection of images by clicking on the "Images" tab in the navigation menu.
  • Use the search bar to find specific images by keyword, category, or photographer.
  • Create an account to purchase images, save favorites, and access exclusive content.

Searching for Images

To search for images, follow these steps:

  • Click on the search bar at the top of the page.
  • Enter your search query, such as a keyword, category, or photographer.
  • Press Enter to view the search results.
  • Use the filters on the left side of the page to narrow down your search results.

Purchasing Images

To purchase an image, follow these steps:

  • Click on the image you want to purchase.
  • Click the "Add to Cart" button.
  • Review your cart and proceed to checkout.
  • Enter your payment information and complete the purchase.

Account Management

To manage your account, follow these steps:

  • Log in to your account by clicking on the "Login" button in the top right corner of the page.
  • Click on your username to access your account dashboard.
  • View your order history, saved favorites, and account settings.

Troubleshooting

If you encounter any issues while using our website, try the following:

  • Check our FAQ page for answers to common questions.
  • Contact our customer support team via email or phone.
  • Try clearing your browser cache and cookies.

FAQ

Q: How do I purchase an image?
A: To purchase an image, click on the image you want to purchase, then click the "Add to Cart" button. Review your cart and proceed to checkout.
Q: How do I create an account?
A: To create an account, click on the "Sign Up" button in the top right corner of the page. Enter your email address, password, and other required information.
Q: What is the refund policy?
A: We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee. If you are not satisfied with your purchase, please contact our customer support team to initiate a refund.
